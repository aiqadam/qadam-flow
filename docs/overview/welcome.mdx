# 🔥 Why Qadam Flow is Different:

- **💖 Loved by Everyone**: Intuitive interface and great experience for both technical and non-technical users with a quick learning curve.
  

![](/resources/templates.gif)
- **🌐 Open Ecosystem:** All qadams are open source. Inherited 715 community integrations from upstream Activepieces — region-specific integrations (Payme, Click, Uzum, Kaspi) are added by the Qadam Flow community.

- **🛠️  Qadams are written in Typescript**: Qadams are npm packages in TypeScript, offering full customization with the best developer experience, including **hot reloading** for **local** qadam development on your machine. 😎


![](/resources/create-action.png)


- **🤖 AI-Ready**: Native AI qadams and agents are built into Qadam Flow. Integrating AI into your flows is seamless and simple—experiment with popular providers, or quickly create custom agents using our easy-to-use AI SDK.

- **🏢 No paywalls**: Features like SSO, RBAC, audit logs, custom branding — all free, all in the open, shipping as production demand requires. See the [Roadmap](https://github.com/aiqadam/qadam-flow/blob/main/ROADMAP.md).

- **🔒 Secure by Design**: Self-hosted and network-gapped for maximum security and control over your data.

- **🧠 Human in Loop**: Delay execution for a period of time or require approval. These are just qadams built on top of the qadam framework, and you can build many qadams like that. 🎨
