## Methods
- [Contribute Back](/build-qadams/sharing-qadams/contribute): Publish your qadam by contributing back your qadam to main repository.
- [Community](/build-qadams/sharing-qadams/community): Publish your qadam on npm directly and share it with the community.
- [Private](/build-qadams/sharing-qadams/private): Publish your qadam on activepieces privately.
